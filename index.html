SKILLS

Tools and Technologies I possess.

Testing & Quality Assurance

  Conduct functional testing across mobile apps, games, and digital products to ensure every feature behaves as expected under normal and edge-case scenarios.

  Perform regression testing to verify bug fixes and prevent recurrence of previous issues. Execute smoke testing on new builds to confirm that core functionality is stable before deeper QA passes.

  Validate features for correct behavior, including user interactions, workflows, and content consistency. Conduct end-to-end testing of user journeys, ensuring seamless navigation, task completion, and expected outcomes.

  Perform cross-device and cross-platform testing to confirm consistent behavior on different Android and iOS devices. Pre-release and post-update testing to identify potential crashes, freezes, or performance issues before release.

  Impact: Helps teams avoid critical bugs, reduces post-release errors, and ensures a smooth, polished experience for end users.

Bug Reporting & Defect Documentation

  Document and communicate reproducible bugs with clear reproduction steps, screenshots, and context for development teams.

  Capture expected vs actual results and classify defects by severity and priority to streamline triage and resolution. Identify and report critical issues, crashes, freezes, or blockers, ensuring nothing impacts release readiness.

  Verify defect fixes, track regression, and maintain comprehensive QA sheets, bug logs, and issue tracking for transparency. Analyze edge cases and user-facing inconsistencies, ensuring a polished and reliable product experience.

Product & Gameplay Validation

  Evaluate feature behavior, progression systems, and user flows to ensure products are intuitive, balanced, and engaging.

  Review win/loss conditions, scoring, reward mechanics, and progression logic to prevent exploitation and enhance retention. Test onboarding flows, tutorials, and help systems to ensure clarity and smooth user adoption.

  Analyze overall product behavior to detect friction points, UX issues, and areas for improvement before release. Collaborate with product and design teams to ensure functionality aligns with business and user experience goals.

QA Skills

  Functional Testing – Verifying that all product features, workflows, and interactions behave as expected across web, mobile, and digital platforms.

  Regression Testing – Ensuring bug fixes and new updates do not introduce regressions or break existing functionality.

  Exploratory Testing – Identifying issues, edge cases, and usability problems by creatively interacting with products beyond defined test cases.

  End-to-End Testing – Validating complete user journeys across key workflows and feature combinations for consistency and correctness.

  Performance & Stability Testing – Monitoring load times, responsiveness, crashes, and memory usage to maintain high-quality, stable releases.

  Cross-Platform & Device Testing – Ensuring consistent functionality, UI/UX, and performance across Android, iOS, web, and different device specifications.

  Bug Reporting & Defect Documentation – Creating detailed, actionable bug reports with steps to reproduce, severity, and priority classification.

  Automation Awareness – Understanding when and how to implement automated testing tools and frameworks to optimize QA coverage and efficiency.

  User Experience (UX) Validation – Reviewing workflows, UI elements, and interaction patterns to ensure intuitive and accessible experiences for end users.

  Test Case & Test Plan Design – Writing structured test cases, test plans, and checklists to ensure full coverage of features and workflows.

  Release Readiness & QA Sign-Off – Verifying builds, updates, or patches meet quality standards before deployment.
